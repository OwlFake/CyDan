I love you, Love love ko!

I keep on saying, "I love you" a lot, I just did, and next to that, I'll follow it up with "I love you more". The thing is, I always wanted to say more. I wanted you to know how when I'm with you, I feel like the world our aircon don't spit condensed water vapor. I feel like the neighbor's dog don't bark at all, I feel like I got everything figured out. I feel, at ease, like my heart is at peace. No minor inconveniences can stop me, and I can push through any problems along the way. All of that, compressed in a single phrase of "I love you" because otherwise, I wouldn't know how to say it all in an instant, without stuttering. If I did, my mind would give the most random stuff that would come to mind. Things like, "you make me feel like wala akong sugat", or " You make me feel like gumagapang ako". Whahahhaa.

Another thing I do is I say "I love you more" instead of "I love you too". I don't just say that kasi competitive ako, I say that kasi I dont want you to think na I feel all of those grandeur feelings only because you love me. I say it with a blatant ignorant of how much you love me and with great understanding of how much I love you. I love you even during days that you cannot give plenty of love. I love you even on days you don't love yourself. I love you even on days na di tayo okay. I love you even on days that I cant give plenty of love, I love you even on days that I dont love myself. I love you kahit okay tayo. There might be certain days someday, where I won't be able to give back a lot of love that you give me, but I most certainly will be giving you all the love I can give. For a simple metaphor, lets say you need 100 pesos from me but I can only give you 10 pesos kasi aun nalang ang pera ko, I'll give you my 10 pesos. So I hope na even on days na magkukulang ako sa pagmamahal ko sayo. Ibibigay ko parin lahat ng pagmamahal ko sayo. Anyways, I love you so much love love ko!!! ♥️♥️♥️
