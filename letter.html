It's just that, one of my anxieties is my inability to provide you with everything you need and want right now. Despite that, I'm doing my best to achieve more in this life so I can give you the life that you deserve. It just so happens kasi na my biggest fear is that I won't be able to give you the life that you wanted. So please be patient with me, my love. Despite being your boyfriend, I'm incredibly limited to what I can do to help you. I can't treat you to expensive dates, I can't get you flowers everytime we meet, and I can't buy you the things you might want, YET. emphasis on that yet part since I know that someday I'll be capable enough to provide you all of that. I'll be capable enough to treat you like an actual princess and I'll be capable enough to actually make your life easy. I'm not there yet but I swear that I will be, so I would love for you to be patient with me.

Do you know why I'm confident that I can do it? Well, you are the reason. I wanna be someone who can stand by your side that you can be fully proud of. I want you to experience the love that you deserve and I want it all to come from me. Greedy na kung greedy, but you inspire me and motivate me so much, and you make me feel love despite everything else. You've given me dreams, hopes, peace even though I'm drowning from pain, despair and chaos. You deserve someone better than me. That's why, I'll be that "someone better than me".

"There is nothing noble in being superior to your fellow man; true nobility is being superior to your former self."

I'll be a better person, a better man, a better partner to you. All because I love you so much, love love ko.
